Importance of the Ritual:

The Vivah Sanskar (marriage ceremony) is one of the most significant Hindu sacraments, uniting two souls and families for a lifetime. It symbolizes a sacred bond, commitment, and the beginning of a new chapter filled with shared responsibilities and joy.

A Suited Shloka:

"Om Sahana Vavatu, Sahanau Bhunaktu, Saha Veeryam Karavavahai, Tejasvina Vaditamastu, Ma Vidvishavahai. Om Shantih Shantih Shantih."
(Meaning: May we be protected together. May we be nourished together. May we work together with great energy. May our intellect be sharpened. May we not hate each other. Om peace, peace, peace.)
